Knowing Vanshaj

Vanshaj Meditech Pvt. Ltd was founded by two passionate engineers turned entrepreneurs, committed to transforming knowledge into practical, high-quality solutions that address societal challenges. Our journey began with a shared vision to leverage our technical expertise and entrepreneurial spirit to create innovative technologies that enhance health and wellbeing.

Driven by our personal experiences and a deep understanding of the issues faced by many in our community, we focus on developing products that make a tangible difference in people’s lives. From improving medication adherence among the elderly to creating user-friendly health monitoring systems, our aim is to bridge the gap between advanced technology and everyday health solutions.

Our Mobile App

The app interface for the Smart Medication Reminder and Monitoring System (SMRMS) is user-friendly and intuitive, designed to facilitate easy registration and medication management. Users can securely create accounts, input detailed medication schedules, and receive reminders. The interface supports medication details including names, dosages, frequencies, and specific intake times, ensuring a comprehensive overview of the user's regimen.
